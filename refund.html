Refund & Cancellation Policy

We aim to provide a smooth and transparent experience. Please review our refund and cancellation guidelines below.

1. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations can be requested before digital access or services are delivered. Once the product is accessed or delivered, cancellation may not be available.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be approved under the following cases:

  • Technical issues preventing access to paid content.
  • Accidental duplicate payment.
  • Service not delivered within the promised time.

All refund requests are reviewed carefully before approval.

3. Non-Refundable Conditions

  • Digital notes already viewed or downloaded.
  • Services already delivered or started.
  • Orders where access has already been provided.

4. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.
