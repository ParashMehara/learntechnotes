Shipping & Delivery Policy

LearnTechNotes provides digital handwritten notes that are delivered instantly through digital methods. Since we do not ship any physical products, all deliveries happen online via secure download links.

1. Delivery Method

After a successful payment, users will receive:

  • An instant download link on the website
  • A download link sent to their email (if applicable)

No physical shipping or courier service is involved.

2. Delivery Time

Delivery is instant. Download links appear immediately after payment and are typically valid for 5 minutes.

3. Failed Delivery

If you do not receive the notes due to technical issues such as:

  • Payment success but link not showing
  • Link expired before download
  • Email not received

You can contact us for support, and we will help you access your purchased notes.

4. No Physical Products

LearnTechNotes does not deliver any printed materials, books, or physical copies of notes. All products are in **digital format (PDF)** only.
